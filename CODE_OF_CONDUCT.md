# Contributor Code of Conduct

We are building the 1Ummah Blockchain to serve the global Muslim community with respect, trust, and shared purpose.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to create a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone, regardless of race, gender, background, or beliefs.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in discussions and feedback
- Use inclusive and professional language
- Accept constructive criticism
- Avoid personal attacks, hate speech, or discrimination

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or threats
- Discriminatory language
- Disrespect towards faith or identity
- Spamming or sabotage
